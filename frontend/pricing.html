Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I run out of credits?

You'll receive a notification when your balance reaches 20% of your last deposit. New indexing jobs won't start with zero credits, but existing jobs will complete. Simply deposit more INC to top up immediately.

How do I get credits?

Connect your wallet and deposit INC tokens. Credits are issued on-chain at a rate of 1 INC = 1,000 credits. Starter accounts receive 1,000 free credits on signup with no deposit required.

Can I get my INC back?

Yes. Unused credits can be withdrawn as INC at any time at the same rate they were deposited. Only credits spent on completed indexing jobs are burned — unspent credits are always yours.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits have no expiry date. Deposit once and use them whenever you need — there are no monthly fees or subscription charges.

Where can I get INC tokens?

INC is an ERC-20 token available on decentralized exchanges. Once acquired, connect your Web3 wallet in the dashboard to deposit and receive platform credits.
